### 反射类基本使用
```shell
include_once "./vendor/autoload.php";
include_once "./Demo.php";
use Roave\BetterReflection\BetterReflection;
$classInfo = (new BetterReflection())
    ->classReflector()
    ->reflect(\Demo\Test::class);
```

#### __toString
将类转换成字符串格式的描述
```php
var_dump($classInfo->__toString());
```
